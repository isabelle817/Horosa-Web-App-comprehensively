# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We want Horosa for Windows to be a respectful, calm, and constructive collaboration space.

Contributors, maintainers, and community members are expected to:

- communicate in good faith
- focus on behavior and impact instead of personal attacks
- welcome different experience levels
- keep technical disagreement grounded in evidence and reproducible facts

## Unacceptable behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment, threats, or discriminatory language
- doxxing or sharing private information without permission
- bad-faith trolling or repeated disruption
- publishing secrets, private keys, or sensitive user data

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or block participation when behavior harms the project or its community.
