## Signature Workflows / 代表性工作流

### Natal to Timing / 从本命到时运

Horosa already supports a continuous predictive flow on Windows: natal and 3D chart reading can lead straight into primary directions, zodiacal releasing, firdaria, profection, solar arc, returns, and annual methods inside the same packaged desktop application.

Windows 版同样已经把“从本命盘进入时运阅读”的路径做成一套连续工作流，而不是单独挂着一组互不相干的方法名称。

### Relationship Analysis / 关系分析

The relationship layer already includes compare, composite, synastry, time-space midpoint, and Marks charts as parallel ways of inspecting the same relationship structure.

关系分析部分也不是单页比较盘，而是比较盘、组合盘、影响盘、时空中点盘、马克斯盘并行组织，用来从不同结构切同一段关系。

### Chinese Traditional Stack / 中国传统术数栈

Bazi, Ziwei, calendar, Feng Shui, and supporting references are already part of the same desktop product, which makes Horosa feel like a broader Chinese traditional stack rather than a single-method release.

八字、紫微斗数、万年历、风水和配套参考入口已经都在同一桌面产品里，因此它呈现出来的更像一整套中国传统术数栈。

### Yi & Sanshi Depth / 易与三式纵深

Yi and Sanshi are represented both as standalone modules and as a deeper integrated surface: Su Zhan, Yi Gua, Liu Ren, Jin Kou, Dun Jia, Tai Yi, Tong She Fa, and Sanshi United with overview, shensha, references, and Bagong detail.

易与三式部分既有单术入口，也有更深的整合分析面，所以首页看到的不该只是“一个安装器仓库”，而应是“交付了一套纵深很强的桌面工作站”。

## Capability Matrix / 功能矩阵

### Western Astrology / 西方占星

From natal charting to predictive timing and relationship analysis, the Windows build already exposes a full multi-layer astrology surface.

- Natal chart and 3D chart
- Primary directions, zodiacal releasing, firdaria, profection, solar arc, returns, annual methods
- Compare, composite, synastry, time-space midpoint, and Marks charts

### Global and Specialty Modules / 全球与专门模块

Horosa goes beyond the standard Western stack and feels broader than a typical packaged desktop release.

- Jieqi charts
- Astrocartography and planetary maps
- Qizheng Siyu, Hellenistic, Indian, and quantitative views

### Chinese Traditional and Divination / 中国传统与术数

The Chinese traditional layer is arranged as a system rather than a loose add-on.

- Bazi, Ziwei, gua-symbol references, twelve-palace tools, and rule references
- Calendar and Feng Shui as first-class modules
- Yi and Sanshi modules: Su Zhan, Yi Gua, Liu Ren, Jin Kou, Dun Jia, Tai Yi, Tong She Fa

### Desktop Workflow / 桌面工作流

The app includes the controls needed for shaping and exporting analysis sessions, not just generating charts.

- Chart configuration, aspect selection, planet selection
- Chart components and utility tools
- AI export and AI export settings
